Easy And Perfect Solution For Your Travelling App

Our project introduces an innovative solution to the challenge of identifying fungi in the wild. By leveraging AI and image recognition, our app allows users to quickly and accurately identify fungi species using their device's camera.

With our user-friendly interface and comprehensive database, navigating the diverse world of mushrooms becomes effortless, empowering users to make informed decisions about their foraging activities.
